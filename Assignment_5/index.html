Vanilla Milkshake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 large scoops (about 1 1/2 c.) vanilla ice cream
  • 1/4 c. milk
  • Whipped Cream
  • Sprinkes
  • Maraschino Cherry

Directions

  1. In a blender, blend together ice cream and milk.
  2. Once smooth and creamy, pour into glass to enjoy
  3. For the ultimate milkshake experience, top with whipped cream, sprinkles, and a cherry
